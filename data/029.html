Apr 14, 1999		 Into obsolete videodisc technology? The RCA Selectavision Videodisc player lives on at www.cedmagic.com. The CED (Capacitance Electronic Disc) was the only stylus-based video tech and struggled to find a market from 1981 to 1986, before being completely given up for dead. The site features more technical specs than you'd probably want to know, plus a nice CED title database, listing everything released in the format.
